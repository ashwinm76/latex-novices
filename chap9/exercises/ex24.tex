The set of linear equations:
\[
    a_ix_i = b_i \qquad \forall i=1,\ldots,n
\]

can be written as a matrix equation:
\[
    \diag(\vec{a})\vec{x} = \vec{b}
\]

where \(\vec{x} = (x_1, \dotsc, x_n)^T\), \(\vec{b} = (b_1, \dotsc, b_n)^T\) and
\[
    \diag(\vec{a}) = 
    \begin{bmatrix}
        a_1 & 0 & \cdots & 0\\
        0 & a_2 & \ddots & \vdots\\
        \vdots & \ddots & \ddots & 0\\
        0 & \cdots & 0 & a_n
    \end{bmatrix}
\]
